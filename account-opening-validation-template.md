**Purpose**Ensure accuracy, completeness, and regulatory readiness before client activation.

**Template**

**Client & Documentation**

*   Client type identified (Individual / Corporate / Institutional)
    
*   Mandatory documents received and verified
    
*   Documentation consistency confirmed (names, identifiers, mandates)
    

**Account Setup**

*   Account structure validated
    
*   Product eligibility confirmed
    
*   Currency, limits, and operating rules reviewed
    

**Mandate & Authority**

*   Signatories verified
    
*   Approval hierarchy validated
    
*   Exceptions documented and approved
    

**Risk & Compliance**

*   KYC / CDD checkpoints completed
    
*   Outstanding issues logged and tracked
    
*   No unresolved onboarding blockers at activation
    

**Activation Readiness**

*   End-to-end validation completed
    
*   Operational teams informed
    
*   Client ready to transact without manual intervention
    

**Common Failure Patterns**

*   Partial documentation accepted under pressure
    
*   Mandates validated too late
    
*   Assumptions made between teams
